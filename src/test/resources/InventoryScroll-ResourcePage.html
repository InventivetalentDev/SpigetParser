InventoryScroll 1.1.0

Get quick access to your whole inventory

  • Overview
  • Updates (1)
  • Reviews (2)
  • Version History
  • Discussion
  1. Tested Minecraft Versions:
    • 1.7
    • 1.8
    • 1.9
    This plugin allows you to swap the items in your hotbar with other items in your inventory.
    Scroll while holding Shift (Sneak) and the item(s) in your hotbar get swapped with the next row in your inventory.




    You can also enable continuous scrolling so you can keep scrolling through your whole inventory



    Permissions
    • inventoryscroll.use - Allows player to use the plugin

    Config
    Code (Text):

    # Skip empty inventory slots and go directly to the next available item
    skipEmpty: true

    # Scroll all Hotbar items, instead of only the selected
    scrollAll: true

    # Instead of holding shift to scroll the rows, automatically scroll to the next row when reaching the start/end of the hotbar (scrollAll should be enabled)
    continuousScrolling: false
